# 1. Introduction

## 1.1 Purpose

This Product Requirements Document (PRD) defines the functional and non-functional requirements for the GO-Farmer platform.

The objective of this document is to ensure that everyone involved in the project understands what needs to be built, why it is being built, and how the platform should behave.

This document serves as the primary reference throughout the development lifecycle of GO-Farmer.

---

## 1.2 Product Overview

GO-Farmer is a digital agriculture logistics platform designed to connect farmers with transport providers through a simple, secure, and transparent system.

The platform enables farmers to register, post transportation requests, book suitable vehicles, and track deliveries. Drivers and transport owners can register themselves, manage their vehicles, and accept transport requests.

The first release (MVP) focuses on solving transportation problems for farmers in rural and semi-rural areas.

---

## 1.3 Objectives

The primary objectives of GO-Farmer are:

- Digitize the transport booking process for farmers.
- Reduce dependency on manual phone calls.
- Connect farmers with nearby transport providers.
- Provide transparent booking records.
- Build trust through verified users and vehicles.
- Create a scalable foundation for future agricultural services.
